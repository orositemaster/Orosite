Hojii Freelance

Freelance jechuun hojii ofii ofiin hojjechuu, abbaa hojii tokko malee tajaajila kennuudha. Interneta fayyadamuun hojii hedduu addunyaa guutuu irraa argachuun ni danda'ama.

1. Maaltu Hojii Freelance Jedhama?

  • Graphic Design
  • Barreeffama fi Qorannoo
  • Translation (Fakkeenyaaf: Afaan Oromo → English)
  • Website Design (HTML, CSS, WordPress)
  • Programming (Python, JavaScript, App)
  • Social Media Management
  • Voice Over fi Podcast

2. Website Hojiif Barbaachisan

  • Fiverr - fiverr.com
  • Upwork - upwork.com
  • Freelancer - freelancer.com
  • PeoplePerHour - peopleperhour.com
  • Toptal - toptal.com

3. Akkaataa Hojii Argachuun Danda'amu

- Akkaata sirrii ta’een profile kee guuti. - Tajaajila ati beekuu danda’u barreessi. - Gaaffii hojii irratti response gaarii kenni. - Hojii siif kennan yeroo irratti fixi. - Rating fi review gaarii argachuun hojii itti fufsiisa.

4. Faayidaa Hojii Freelance

  • Yeroo ofiin to’achuu dandeessa
  • Biyya kam irraa iyyuu hojjachuu dandeessa
  • Galii dabalataa argachuun ni danda’ama
  • Ogummaa kee gabbifachuu fi hirmaannaa addunyaa keessaa

5. Xumura

Hojii freelance Afaan Oromoo fi Afaanota biroo irratti cimsuun carraa hojii fi galii guddaa argamsiisa. Ogeessa ta’i, hin quufin, hojii kee babal’isi!
