Barnoota Salphaa

Akkaataa Galii Online argachuu fi Teknoolojii Afaan Oromootti Barachuu

Gosa Barnoota

  • YouTube: Viidiyoo barreessuu, edit gochuu, AdSense irraa bu'aa argachuu
  • Telegram: Group/Channel bananii odeeffannoo tamsaasuu fi bu'aa argachuu
  • Website: Blogger ykn GitHub Pages fayyadamuun website uumuu
  • App Mobile: App akka TaskMate, Sweatcoin fi Fundi fayyadamuun galii argachuu
  • Facebook Page: Fayyadamtoota walitti qabuun tajaajila dhiheessuu
  • TikTok: Video gabaabaa barreessuun followers fi sponsorship argachuu

Barnoota Teknoolojii Salphaa

  • HTML/CSS: Akkamitti fuula website salphaa ijaaruu dandeenyu
  • Canva: Suuraawwan, poster fi design salphaa hojjechuu
  • Google Drive & Docs: Document, link fi PDF qopheessuu fi hiruu
  • Typing Afaan Oromoo: Afaan keenya irratti barreessuu fi qopheessuu

Gorsa Dabalataa

Barnoota online irraa argamu marti bu’aa fidu danda’a. Qabxiin guddaan: carraa beekuu, hojjechuu fi obsaan itti fufuu dha. Orosite keessatti barnoonni siif salphaa ta’ee qophaa’a.
